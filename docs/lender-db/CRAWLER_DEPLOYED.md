## 📊 Human-Readable Report

The crawler now includes a **human-readable report** in the response:

### Report Sections

1. **Summary**
   - Total crawled
   - Successfully updated
   - Business lending found
   - Errors
   - Skipped

2. **Business Lending Discovered**
   - List of lenders with business lending
   - Loan types found
   - Services mentioned

3. **Successfully Updated**
   - Lenders that were updated
   - Fields that were updated
   - Website information found

4. **Errors**
   - Lenders that had errors
   - Error messages

5. **No Website URL**
   - Lenders without website URLs

6. **Statistics**
   - Success rate
   - Business lending rate
   - Error rate

7. **Next Steps**
   - Action items based on results

## 🔍 What Gets Crawled

### Business Lending Detection
- Searches for 13+ business lending keywords
- Detects SBA loans, commercial loans, equipment financing, etc.
- Extracts loan types and requirements

### Public Information
- Website title and metadata
- Phone numbers (gated)
- Email addresses (gated)
- Services mentioned (FHA, VA, DSCR, etc.)
- States mentioned

### Data Storage
- All data stored in gated JSONB fields
- Never displayed in public CMS
- Only accessible through broker portal
